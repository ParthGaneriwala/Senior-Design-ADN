Mission Statement

To provide an automated computer controlled flight-traversing drone that can travel and land amongst aerial traffic safely and reliably without an operator.

Project Goal

Our task is to provide automated navigation in the event of a total loss of connection with the goal of returning back to designated landing site safely while navigating densely populated cities and aerospace.
